How we work

Four steps, no surprises

  1. Site visit

    60–90 minutes

    We walk the space with you, take measurements, and talk honestly about what your budget buys. Free, no obligation.

  2. Written quote

    Within 3 business days

    A fixed-price quote with line items and allowances spelled out. No "TBD" rows, no verbal ballparks.

  3. Build with weekly updates

    Per the quoted schedule

    One lead carpenter owns your job. Every Friday you get photos, what happened, and what happens next week.

  4. Final walkthrough & warranty

    About 1 hour, then 5 years

    We walk every room together and fix the punch list before final payment. Then the 5-year workmanship warranty starts, in writing.
